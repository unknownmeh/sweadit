About Aetheria Publishing

What we are and what we provide

Aetheria Publishing is a literary publishing platform registered and operating from Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. Our registered address is 4th Floor, Literary House, Beach Road, Visakhapatnam – 530001.

Our Service

Aetheria provides an online platform that enables:

  • Authors to upload, publish, and distribute their novels, books, and written works at no cost.
  • Readers to discover, read, and engage with published works.
  • Optional editorial feedback, cover design resources, and formatting assistance.
  • An optional royalty programme for authors whose works are accessed by premium subscribers.

Core promise: Publishing on Aetheria will always be free for authors. We may introduce premium reader features in the future, but the author publishing pipeline will remain free of charge.

Platform Status

Aetheria is a platform (intermediary) as defined under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and IT (Intermediary Guidelines) Rules, 2021. We do not claim editorial control over user-published works and act as a conduit for author expression.

Content Standards & Submission Rules

What may and may not be published on Aetheria

Permitted Content

We welcome fiction and non-fiction across all genres — romance, mystery, science fiction, literary fiction, fantasy, memoir, poetry, self-help, children's literature, academic writing, and more — in any language.

Content You Must NOT Publish

Illegal content: Any material that violates Indian law or the laws of the reader's jurisdiction, including content that promotes terrorism, incites violence, or constitutes hate speech under IPC sections 153A, 295A, or 505.

Child exploitation material: Any content that sexualises, exploits, or endangers minors. Zero-tolerance policy with immediate removal and reporting to authorities.

Plagiarism & copyright infringement: You must own or have explicit rights to all content you publish. Uploading others' work without permission is prohibited and may result in legal action.

Defamatory content: False statements of fact presented as true that damage the reputation of identifiable real individuals.

Spam or AI-generated bulk content: Mass-generated, low-quality, or duplicate content uploaded for SEO or commercial manipulation rather than genuine literary purpose.

Malware or harmful code: Files embedded with scripts, executables, or any code designed to harm readers' devices.

Content Moderation

We review flagged content within 72 hours. Authors whose content is removed will be notified with reasons and given 14 days to appeal. Repeated violations result in progressive sanctions up to permanent account termination.

Mature content (adult themes, graphic violence) must be clearly marked with appropriate content warnings during submission. We reserve the right to add or enforce content warnings on any published work.

Royalties & Payment Terms

How the optional royalty programme works

The royalty programme is entirely optional. Publishing and distributing your work on Aetheria is and will remain free regardless of whether you participate in the royalty programme.

How Royalties Work

Authors who opt into the royalty programme earn revenue based on page-reads by premium subscribers. The royalty pool is funded by premium reader subscription fees and distributed monthly based on each author's share of total platform reads.

Termination by Aetheria

1

Warning: For first-time minor violations, we issue a written warning with details of the breach and required corrective action.

2

Suspension: Repeated or serious violations result in temporary account suspension (7–30 days) with notification and right to appeal.
