What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Everything we build flows from these four principles.

Radical Openness

Publishing should have no toll booths. We charge authors nothing — zero. Our platform is and always will be free to use for every writer on earth.

Author First

Authors retain full creative control and all rights to their work. We are a platform, not a publisher — you own your story, always.

Community Built

Readers and writers grow together. Reviews, reading lists, and a curated discovery engine help great stories find the right eyes.

Long-Term Vision

We are building infrastructure for literature — not for profit. Sustained by a mission, not a marketplace. Your stories fund nothing but culture.
